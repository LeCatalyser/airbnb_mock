Sunset Bike Ride
Tokyo Cruise through Old Tokyo with a Cycling Pro
3 hour sports experience
Hosted by Bradley
About your host, Bradley
Brad, aka "Racer X Tokyo," is an outdoor guide and educator who has lived in Tokyo for 20 years. He's a member of the Cycling Embassy of Japan, an advocacy group promoting infrastructure for cyclists.
About your host, Bradley
Brad, aka "Racer X Tokyo," is an outdoor guide and educator who has lived in Tokyo for 20 years. He's a member of the Cycling Embassy of Japan, an advocacy group promoting infrastructure for cyclists.
What well do
See the last vestiges of old Tokyo tucked away in the city's west side. At sunset, we'll pedal along the shrines and temples lining the historic Kanda River, catching a glimpse of locals returning home and (if we're lucky) Mt. Fuji in the distance. We'll break for a stroll and refreshments in a funky arts district before cruising back to my studio. Optional: Gyoza dinner & hot spring bath.

Where well be
The Kanda River, famous for supplying Edo with its water. Lined with historic sites and infrastructure, attractions include Meiji University and Koshu Kaido, a road that extends all the way to Osaka. Following the Keio Line train tracks, our last stop is Shimokitazawa, a vibrant arts district.
